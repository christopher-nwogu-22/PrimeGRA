AIM

The aim of the prime cooperative society is to promote and support members as a means of addressing poverty, social exclusion and financial inequality in our community or society.

MISSION

Our vision is to be the preferred and leading cooperative in the south west region. It is also to grow into a microfinance bank.

VISION

Our vision is to be the preferred and leading cooperative in the south west region. It is also to grow into a microfinance bank.

OBJECTIVE

Our objectives is to lend money, provide goods and services to members and thus enable them to attain increased income and savings, investment, productivity and purchasing power and also to promote equitable distribution of net surplus. We shall provide maximum economic benefits to our members, teaching them efficient ways of doing business, propagate cooperative practices and new ideas in business management and allow the lower income groups to increase their ownership in the wealth of the nation. We will embark on property businesses that will be of mutual benefits to the members and the cooperative.

CORE VALUES

Prime cooperative is based on the value of Passion, Respect, Integrity, Motivation/Mentoring and Excellence.
