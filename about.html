ABOUT PRIME

PMCS is a community-driven cooperative dedicated to empowering our members through collaborative savings and support programs designed to uplift and improve livelihoods. Our mission is to create a sustainable financial support system that caters to the needs of our members and fosters a thriving network of mutual growth.


President Dr.Helen nwobodo

Aim

The aim of prime cooperative society is to promote and support members as a means of addressing poverty, social exclusion and financial inequality in our community or society.

Vision

Our vision is to be the preferred and leading cooperative in the region (South West). It is also to grow into a micro-finance Bank.

Mision

We strive to be a successful cooperative working towards creating a better, fairer world and to enhance the lives of our members, customers and the communities we serve.

Objective

Our objective is to lend money, provide goods and services to members and thus enable them to attain increased income and savings, investment, productivity and purchasing power and also to promote equitable distribution of net surplus.
We shall provide maximum economic benefits to our members, teaching them efficient ways of doing business, propagate cooperative practices and new ideas in business management and allow the lower income groups to increase their ownership in the wealth of the nation.
We will embark on property businesses that will be of mutual benefits to the members and the cooperative.

Core Values

Prime cooperative is based on the value of Passion, Respect, Integrity, Motivation / mentoring and Excellence.
